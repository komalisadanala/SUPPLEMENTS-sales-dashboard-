# SUPPLEMENTS-sales-analysis

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of supplements sales performance, combining key business metrics, product insights, and channel analysis into a single interactive view.The dashboard is designed as a high-level performance monitoring tool, helping users quickly understand sales trends, profitability, and product performance at a glance.

### Dataset Information

The dataset used for this analysis contains the following 9 columns:
1. Product Name
2. Product Category
3. Units Sold
4. Revenue
5. Profit
6. Returns
7. Channel
8. Date
9. Customer Rating

## Dashboard Insights

   - **Overall Business Performance**: The dashboard shows strong overall performance with 872K revenue and 354K profit, indicating healthy profitability across products.
   - **Top Performing Product**: Multivitamin Gummies is the highest-selling product, especially dominating in the offline channel, making it the key revenue driver.
   - **Low Performing Products**: Products like Wormwood Liquid show comparatively lower sales, indicating potential areas for improvement.
   - **Channel Performance**: Offline sales outperform online sales overall, suggesting that the business relies more on traditional sales channels.
   - **Online Sales Leader**: Vitamin D3+K2 performs best in the online channel, indicating strong digital demand for this product.
   - **Return Analysis**: The presence of returns (26.20) indicates some level of product return activity that may need monitoring.

## Conclusion
  The dashboard provides a clear and comprehensive view of supplements sales performance, highlighting key trends in revenue, profit, and product demand. It reveals that a few products, such as Multivitamin Gummies and Vitamin D3+K2, drive the majority of sales, while offline channels contribute more significantly to overall revenue.The analysis also shows a strong relationship between units sold and profit, indicating consistent business growth with increased sales volume. Additionally, variations in product performance across channels suggest opportunities for targeted sales strategies.Overall, this dashboard helps in identifying top-performing products, understanding channel effectiveness, and supporting data-driven decision-making to improve business performance.
